FAQ

What is a Free Fire EAT Token?

EAT (External Auth Token) is obtained after logging in via Garena OAuth. It appears in your browser history as ?eat= in the redirect URL.

What is a JWT Token?

JWT (JSON Web Token) is a secure token format required by Free Fire game servers to authenticate API requests.

Is this safe to use?

Yes, 100% safe. Login goes through Garena's official OAuth — your password is never shared with us.

Which regions are supported?

All major regions: Indonesia (ID), India (IND), Singapore (SG), Brazil (BR), Thailand (TH), Vietnam (VN) and more.
