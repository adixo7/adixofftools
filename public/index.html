Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Free Fire EAT Token?

EAT (External Auth Token) is an authentication token used by Free Fire/Garena to verify your account. It's obtained after logging in through Garena OAuth and is found in the browser history URL.

What is a Free Fire JWT Token?

JWT (JSON Web Token) is a secure token format derived from your EAT or Access Token. It's required by Free Fire servers to authenticate API requests.

Is this Free Fire Token Generator safe?

Yes, 100% safe. We use the official Garena OAuth login — your password is never shared with us. We only convert the token you already have.

How to get my Free Fire EAT token?

Log in using the provider buttons above, then check your browser history for URLs containing ?eat= or /callback/?eat=. Usually select the second URL from the top.

Which Free Fire regions are supported?

All major regions are supported: Indonesia (ID), India (IND), Singapore (SG), Brazil (BR), Thailand (TH), Vietnam (VN) and more.
